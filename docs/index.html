Step information

For local rendering and rotation animation, you may want to provide what is the amount you want to change a given parameter.
To do so, just provide the step information for those parameters with the attribute data-step="azimuth=2&dolly=5"

Toggle interaction mode

When a viewer is used with data-no-ui, data-no-mouse and data-animation, you may feel helpless if you want to use the actual control panel to tune some parameter or the view. To do so, you will just need to double click on the view to toggle the current mode you are in. This will stop the animation and you will gain back the mouse interaction.
